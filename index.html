About TEE'S DESSERT STREET

Founded with love and passion in 2020, our bakery is a celebration of flavors, textures, and the art of dessert-making. What began as a home kitchen experiment has blossomed into a beloved destination for those who appreciate handcrafted sweetness.

Every creation is a masterpiece, blending traditional techniques with modern flair. We source only the finest ingredients, from organic flours to premium chocolates, ensuring each bite is a moment of pure indulgence.
